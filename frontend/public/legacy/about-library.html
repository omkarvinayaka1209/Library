The Heart of Academic VEMU

The Central Library of VEMU Institute of Technology stands as an integrated knowledge resource centre that supports learning, teaching, research, and academic excellence across the campus. It provides a welcoming environment where students and faculty can read, explore, and strengthen subject knowledge.

With organized collections, circulation support, and a dedicated digital learning environment, the library helps users access books, journals, and online academic content efficiently. It encourages disciplined study, self-learning, and research-oriented thinking.

The library also supports classroom learning, project work, seminar preparation, and competitive exam study by offering access to digital facilities and useful e-resources.

Library Facilities & Timings
